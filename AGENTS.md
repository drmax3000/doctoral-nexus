# Document Processing Rigor (No Blind Spots)

When instructed to process, extract, or analyze an entire book, document, or sequence, **NEVER skip chapters, files, or sections based on assumptions of relevance or past context.** Always process the entire requested scope linearly and exhaustively. Avoid blind spots caused by assuming the user already has the knowledge or that some sections are "less relevant". If a task is too large, schedule it completely via background scripts rather than arbitrarily filtering the scope.
